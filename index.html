Optimization Tips

☀️
Peak Hours in Arusha
Best solar production: 9 AM – 3 PM. Run heavy loads (charging, pumps) during this window for maximum savings.
🧹
Clean Your Panel
Dust can reduce output by up to 20%. Wipe with a damp cloth every 2 weeks, especially in dry season.
🌡️
Temperature Management
Ensure 10–15 cm air gap below the panel for cooling. Output drops ~0.5% per °C above 25°C.
📐
Optimal Panel Angle
At Arusha's latitude (−3°), tilt your panel 5–10° facing north for maximum annual energy yield.
🔋
Battery Health
Avoid draining battery below 50% regularly. This can more than double your battery's lifespan.
🔌
Check Connections Monthly
Loose MC4 connectors and corroded terminals are the leading cause of open-circuit faults. Inspect monthly.
